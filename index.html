Welcome to ChiChi Squirell Knits!

ChiChi Squirrel Knits is a wonderland for every knitter - from a beginner to a pro. We carefully crafted this website to help you discover endless possibilities of this art and support you on every step of your knitting journey.

Are you curious about knitting and wondering where to start? Go straight to our How to start page to make your first knitting stitch-steps.

Want to dive further into the depth of knitting techniques or refresh that confusing stitch you need for your project? Head over to the Knitting Secrets page and follow our step-by-step tutorials!

Are you an experienced knitter and want to learn how to adjust existing patterns or to create your own ones? We got you covered! Follow the path to the Get Creative! page and boost your creative skills!

If you are here for a casual browse or an inspiration, we highly recommend check out the ChiChi projects page, where you can find your new project based on your knitting skill level!

Have any questions on the topic or suggestions how can we get better? Contact us! We will be happy to hear from you!

We are excited for your adventure! Good luck!
