Knitting Projects

There are so many things you can knit! We are happy to offer you some knitting projects depending on your skill level and experience.

Each project has an indicator of a difficulty level, which you can rely on when choosing a project based on how confident you are with required techniques. However, we don’t want you to think that if you are a beginner, you cannot complete an intermediate project. We encourage learning by doing, so feel free to check all the suggested projects and don’t be afraid to try new things. In any case, you always can head over to the Knitting Secrets page and learn the required techniques as you go.

We offer you knitting projects of 4 levels of difficulty: beginner, easy, intermediate, experienced.

Beginner

Projects for first-time knitters using basic knit and purl stitches. Minimal shaping.

Easy

Projects using basic stitches, repetitive stitch patterns, simple color changes, and simple shaping and finishing.

Intermediate

Project with a variety of stitches, such as basic cables, and lace, simple intarsia, double-pointed needles techniques, mid level shaping and finishing.

Experienced

Projects using advanced techniques and stitches, such as short rows, fair isle, more intricate intarsia, cables, lace pattern , and numerous color changes.
